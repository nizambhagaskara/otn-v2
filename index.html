GeForce RTX 5090

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090 is the top-tier GPU in the RTX 50 series, built on the Blackwell architecture with 32 GB of GDDR7 memory. It excels at 4K and 8K gaming, 3D rendering, and AI tasks, offering major improvements in ray tracing, AI-based rendering, and efficiency. With support for DLSS 4 and Multi-Frame Generation, it boosts frame rates dramatically, making it the most powerful consumer GPU for gamers, creators, and developers needing extreme performance and ultra-realistic visuals.
courtesy image of NVIDIA

Unreal Engine 5

Unreal Engine 5 introduces Lumen for real-time global illumination and Nanite for ultra-detailed assets, making cinematic-quality environments possible in real-time. Its MetaHuman framework allows realistic haracters with lifelike animations, bridging the gap between movies and games. UE5 sets the new standard for next-generation visuals and performance across all platforms.
